How this works

This is not a course with a deadline

It is a permanent recovery resource — built to be used at your pace, returned to as needed, and referenced for as long as recovery continues.

Lifetime access to everything

All 20 chapters, 100+ lessons, audio discussions, worksheets, and future updates are yours permanently. Nothing expires.

Fully self-paced

There is no schedule to keep up with. Move through chapters at whatever speed fits your energy, your situation, and your recovery.

Recovery timelines are personal

Every stroke is different. Every person is different. This system meets you where you are — not where a fixed schedule expects you to be.

A reference library for the long run

Many participants explore the full material within a few weeks — then return to specific chapters as their recovery evolves. The platform is designed for that.
